# Season27 brand assets

The Season27 mark combines a traditional association-football panel ball, the number 27, and
subtle league-table position lines. The round black-and-white football makes the English football
context explicit and avoids American-football or US-franchise visual cues.

- `season27-logo-source.png` is the original generated raster artwork.
- `season27-logo-full.png` is the full-resolution transparent master.
- `season27-logo.png` is the transparent production asset.
- `season27-logo-light.png` and `season27-logo-dark.png` are named variants for explicit use on
  contrasting surfaces.
- `favicon.ico`, `favicon-32.png`, and `apple-touch-icon.png` are browser/device derivatives.

Generation used the built-in image-generation tool with a flat magenta chroma-key background,
followed by local chroma-key removal and Pillow resizing. The prompt requested a crisp,
vector-friendly English association-football mark with a classic round panel ball, the number 27,
and league-table lines in forest green, off-white and lime. It explicitly excluded American
footballs, helmets, goalposts, yard lines, varsity typography and copyrighted league symbols.
